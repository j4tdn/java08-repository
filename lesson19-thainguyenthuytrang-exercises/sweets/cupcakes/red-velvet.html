Red velvet cupcake

A cupcake (also known as a fairy cake) is a small cake designed to be eatn by one person, baked in a thin paper wrapper, and decorated with frosting on top.

One of the mainstays at Coffee & Cake is our famous Red Velvet Cupcake. A well-guarded recipe combined with natural ingredient make this little rascal unapologetically delicious. Topped with decorative vanilla frosting, the Red Velvet Cupcake is a favorite among kids as well.
