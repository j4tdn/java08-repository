Red velvet

A cupcake (also known as a fairy cake) is a small cake designed to be eaten by one person, baked in a thin paper wrapper, and decorated with frosting on top.

Coffee & Cake is our famous Red Velvet Cupcake. A well-guarded recipe combined with natural ingredients make this little red rascal unapologetically delicious. Topped with the decorative vanilla frosting, the Red Velvet Cupcake is a favorite among kids as well.
